EncryptFire is a standalone tool you run directly in your browser. It‚Äôs just HTML + JavaScript with no servers involved, so nothing ever leaves your computer. You can even use it fully offline on an air-gapped machine.

How it works:

  1. A cipher map ‚Äî a scrambled codebook of every possible seed word (the entire BIP-39 wordlist of 2,048 words).
  2. A short list of numbers that point to your actual seed words inside that map.
